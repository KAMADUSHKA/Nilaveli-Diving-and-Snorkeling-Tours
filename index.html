About Us

Welcome to Nilaveli Diving & Snorkeling Tours, your gateway to unforgettable underwater adventures in Nilaveli, Sri Lanka. We are passionate about delivering the best diving and snorkeling experiences while ensuring safety, comfort, and excitement for all our guests.

  • Safety First

    Your safety is our top priority. Our experienced, certified dive instructors and guides follow international safety standards to provide a secure and enjoyable experience.

  • New & Well-Maintained Equipment

    We use the latest diving and snorkeling gear to enhance your experience. Whether you're diving deep or snorkeling in shallow waters, our state-of-the-art gear ensures maximum comfort and performance.

  • Explore the Beauty of Nilaveli

    Join us to discover the breathtaking marine life and vibrant coral reefs of Nilaveli. Whether you're an experienced diver or a first-time snorkeler, we have tailored tours that suit all skill levels. Our friendly and knowledgeable team is here to guide you every step of the way.
